Projects

Axis Bank Student Savings Scheme Analysis

Led a comprehensive business analysis for Axis Bank's Student Savings Scheme, a fully digital savings platform designed for minors (ages 3-8). Identified key customer pain points in existing minor savings options, including mandatory branch visits and extensive paperwork. Developed requirements for a seamless digital onboarding process that could be completed within 5 minutes, with structured deposit plans starting at just INR 500. The solution integrated with Axis Bank's existing digital infrastructure, complied with RBI regulations, and featured competitive interest rates with tax benefits. This initiative successfully improved customer retention by targeting a younger demographic while promoting digital adoption among existing account holders.(Training project - FCD)

Amigo Internal App for Branch Users

Conducted business analysis for 'Amigo,' a centralized web-based platform designed for UAE bank branch staff to streamline customer onboarding, money transfers, and forex transactions. Identified critical issues in the existing process including lack of centralization, high fraud risk, transaction delays, and limited reporting visibility. Developed requirements for a comprehensive solution featuring integrated KYC verification, real-time processing with compliance checks, and role-based security protocols. The implemented system significantly reduced transaction processing time, enhanced fraud prevention mechanisms, and improved customer satisfaction through streamlined service delivery while maintaining strict compliance with UAE banking regulations.(Training project - FCD)
