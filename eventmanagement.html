Event Management

Startup Maanadu - A Mega Event

On January 25th, 2024, I and along with collaborative efforts successfully orchestrated Startup Manadu at Siruseri, Sipcot IT Park, attracting over 500 attendees and featuring 20 innovative startups. Credit to comprehensive management which brought to life a vibrant platform for meaningful conversations. The event highlighted inspiring keynote speakers, dynamic pitch sessions, and live product demonstrations that engaged participants. As a result, all the stall owners generated business with participants and investors, fostering collaborations that significantly impact the startup ecosystem. This event not only showcased innovation but also created a lasting network of industry experts and entrepreneurs, marking a pivotal moment for all involved.

Spice Up TN - A Buyer-Seller Meet

On April 6th, 2024, through collaborative efforts, we successfully organized Spice Up TN at Siruseri OMR, attracting over 450 attendees and featuring 25 small-scale food and agri-based entrepreneurs. Thanks to our seamless teamwork and effective management, we created a dynamic marketplace that facilitated over 150 business transactions, resulting in an estimated 25% increase in revenue for participating vendors. The event emphasized meaningful interactions between buyers and sellers, showcasing innovative products and services in the food and agriculture sector. This not only fostered valuable partnerships but also strengthened the local economy, marking a significant milestone for all participants involved.
